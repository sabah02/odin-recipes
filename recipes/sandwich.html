Cheese Sandwich

Description

Every homosapiens love cheese and sandwich. These are the easiest to prepare and eat. I love these becasue when I am bored and tired I have to just get some cheese, veggies and bread and of course a gas stove. You can add your favourite ingredient anything goes with cheese right. Just don't burn the bread. It does not even require prerequisite, even a newbie can make it.

So let's Start

Ingredients

  • Bread (Of your choice)
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (Of your choice
  • Butter
  • Sauces
  • Oregano
  • Chilli Flakes

Steps:

  1. Take bread slices
  2. Spread butter on them
  3. Keep a pan on the gas on low flame
  4. Add some butter
  5. Add chopped onions or other veggies you want in your sandwich
  6. Add sauces of your choice
  7. Saute the veggies until they are semi-cooked
  8. Add Oregano and chilli flakes
  9. Turn off the gas add spread the prepared stuffing on one slice of bread
  10. Add any amount of cheese you like on the other bread
  11. Make the sandwich
  12. Toast it if you like for 2-3 min on medium flame.

Voila! Your Sandwich Is Ready
