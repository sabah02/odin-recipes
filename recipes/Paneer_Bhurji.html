Paneer Bhurji

Description

Paneer Bhurji is a spicy, healty and tasty recipe for paneer lovers.It contains panner

Ingredients

  • Paneer/Cottage cheese (200 gm)
  • Chilli powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Chilli powder
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Green Chillies
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Ginger Garlic paste

Steps

  1. Add 3-4 tbsp of oil in the pan
  2. Add chopped onions. Saute it for 3-4 mins
  3. Add 1/2 tbsp of coriander powder, 3/4 tbsp of chilli powder and 1/2 tbsp of Turmeric powder
  4. Add 2 tbsp of ginder garlic paste
  5. Cook it for 7-8 mins
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and 4-5 chilles
  7. Cook it for 5 mins
  8. Smash the panner in a bowl and add it to the masala
  9. Mix it all up
  10. Add Salt according to the taste
  11. Cook it for 7-8 mins
  12. Add coriander to garnish

Your Panner Bhurji is Ready to Eat
