Mojito

Description

Do you want a chilled natural flavoured drink for the summers. Then Mojito can be your drink.

Ingredients

  • Any drink with fizz (preferably sprite)
  • Soda
  • 1-2 Lemons
  • Mint leaves
  • Any flavoured juice
  • Sugar
  • Black salt

Steps:

  1. Crush a lime and 10-15 mint leaves.
  2. Add soda and sprite equally to your glass
  3. Add some flavoured juice
  4. Add the crushed lime and mint leaves to the glass
  5. Add a pinch of salt and sugar according to your taste
  6. Garnish your drink with some mint leaves.

Your Drink is Ready
